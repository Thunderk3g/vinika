# approach.md — slot `vk-approach`

**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 (portrait)
**Use:** "How Vinika works" hero still. An amber circle (`#e0a045`) sits behind it and a play-button
overlay sits on top — so **leave calm negative space** (upper area or one side), keep the frame
uncluttered, and avoid putting the key face dead-center where the play button lands.

> Reference the **House Style block** in `README.md` for the full grade/lighting/avoid language.

## Variant A — candid strategy session, wide breathing room

Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:5 portrait orientation, of a small group of consultants and
founders mid strategy session inside a bright modern studio office. A diverse trio — a Black woman in
her 30s gesturing toward a glass wall, an older South Asian man listening intently, a younger
white colleague taking notes — caught candid and unposed, none looking at the camera. They are
clustered in the lower-right third of the frame, leaving generous calm empty space in the upper-left
(soft out-of-focus wall and window light) for an overlay. Soft directional daylight from a large
window, gentle filmic grain, shot on a full-frame 35mm prime at f/2, shallow but controlled depth of
field. Desaturated editorial color grade: deep navy shadows (`#07254c`), warm cream and amber
highlights (`#f5e1c8` / `#e0a045`) catching the window side, restrained slightly-cool mids; wardrobe
in navy, cream and muted teal. Confident, calm, collaborative mood. Negative space kept clean.
**Avoid:** visible text, logos, watermarks, on-screen UI, slides with words, distorted hands,
oversaturated colors, harsh flash, cluttered desks, posed stock smiles, eye contact with camera.

## Variant B — over-the-shoulder at the whiteboard, vertical air

Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:5 portrait orientation. Over-the-shoulder candid of two founders
working at a large glass whiteboard, seen from behind and slightly to the side so faces are mostly
turned away. One reaches up mid-gesture; sticky notes and clean abstract diagrams (no legible text)
on the glass. The pair sits low in the frame; the top half is calm — soft daylight haze and a clean
out-of-focus office, intentional negative space for an overlaid circle and play button. Soft window
light raking from the right, subtle film grain, full-frame 50mm prime at f/2.2, controlled shallow
depth. Muted Vinika grade — navy and ink shadows (`#0a3165` / `#0a0f1a`), warm amber rim light on
shoulders and hair, cream wall highlights, cool muted mids; clothing navy, charcoal, cream. Focused,
purposeful, premium feel. **Avoid:** legible text or words on the whiteboard, logos, watermarks,
distorted hands, neon or oversaturated color, harsh flash, busy clutter, posed grins, camera mugging.

## Variant C — quiet decisive moment, single calm subject

Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:5 portrait orientation. A composed founder in her 40s (Middle
Eastern, natural styling) pauses mid-thought at a meeting table, fingertips together, gaze off-frame
toward a window — a candid decisive beat rather than a pose. Two colleagues sit softly out of focus
beside her. She is placed to one side (rule-of-thirds), with a large area of calm, softly-lit empty
space opposite for the amber circle and play button. Soft overcast daylight, delicate film grain,
full-frame 85mm prime at f/2, creamy controlled bokeh. Desaturated editorial grade: deep navy
shadows, warm cream/amber highlight on her face and the tabletop, muted cool mids, a hint of teal in
the background; wardrobe cream blazer over navy. Calm, confident, human, aspirational. Lots of
negative space. **Avoid:** text, logos, watermarks, screen UI, distorted hands, oversaturated tones,
harsh flash, clutter, stock-photo posing, direct eye contact.
