# results-strip.md — slots `vk-strip-0` … `vk-strip-13` (14 cells)

**Aspect ratio:** 4:5 (portrait)
**Use:** A horizontally-scrolling strip of founder / team / client portraits and workplace moments.
All 14 cells must feel like **one cohesive shoot** — same grade, same light, same lens — but **not
identical**. Use the **Portrait Recipe** as the fixed base, then swap in one **Subject** sentence per
cell. There are 8 subject prompts below (`vk-strip-0` … `vk-strip-7`); for the remaining 6 cells
(`vk-strip-8` … `vk-strip-13`), reuse subjects with a different person (age/ethnicity/gender) and a
fresh angle — the recipe keeps them unified.

> Reference the **House Style block** in `README.md`. Keep the recipe pinned while you iterate so every
> cell matches.

## Portrait Recipe (fixed base — keep identical for all 14 cells)

> Cinematic editorial photograph, **4:5 portrait orientation**, candid and unposed, documentary feel.
> Soft directional natural window light (large window or overcast daylight), gentle filmic grain,
> natural true-to-life skin texture. Full-frame camera, **50mm prime at f/2**, shallow but controlled
> depth of field, soft out-of-focus modern-office background. **Color grade (identical across the set):**
> desaturated editorial — deep navy shadows (`#07254c` / `#0a3165`), near-black ink blacks (`#0a0f1a`),
> warm cream and amber highlights (`#f5e1c8` / `#e0a045`) on skin, restrained slightly-cool mids,
> occasional muted teal (`#3f7d86`) or mist blue-grey (`#b9c6d0`) in the background. Wardrobe limited
> to navy, cream, charcoal, muted teal and warm neutrals. Confident, human, premium, aspirational mood.
> Subject placed slightly off-center with calm negative space. **Avoid:** visible text, logos,
> watermarks, screen UI, distorted hands, plastic AI skin, oversaturated or neon color, harsh flash,
> stiff posed corporate headshot energy, everyone staring straight into the camera, cluttered desks.

To build a cell: paste the Portrait Recipe, then append one Subject line below.
