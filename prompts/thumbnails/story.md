## Variant A — founder portrait, warm and grounded

Cinematic editorial portrait photograph, 3:4 portrait orientation, of a single Vinika founder — a
confident woman in her late 30s, South Asian, natural minimal styling, slight knowing half-smile,
seated on the edge of a desk in a sunlit modern office. Candid editorial framing, three-quarter body,
relaxed posture, hands easy. Soft directional window light from camera-left wrapping her face, gentle
filmic grain, full-frame 85mm prime at f/1.8, creamy controlled bokeh of a warm office behind. Muted
Vinika color grade: deep navy shadows (`#07254c`), warm cream and amber highlights (`#f5e1c8` /
`#e0a045`) on skin and hair, restrained cool mids; wardrobe a cream blazer over navy top. Warm,
authentic, premium, approachable mood. Negative space on one side for layout. **Avoid:** visible text,
logos, watermarks, distorted hands, plastic AI skin, oversaturated color, harsh flash, stiff posed
corporate headshot energy.

## Variant B — the team together, candid documentary

Cinematic editorial photograph, 3:4 portrait orientation, of the Vinika team — a diverse group of
four or five consultants of mixed ages and ethnicities — gathered loosely near a large studio window,
mid warm conversation, a couple laughing genuinely, others listening. Candid documentary framing,
unposed, layered depth with some figures softly out of focus. Soft overcast daylight flooding from the
window, subtle film grain, full-frame 35mm prime at f/2.2. Desaturated editorial grade: navy and ink
shadows (`#0a3165` / `#0a0f1a`), warm amber and cream highlights on faces, muted cool mids, a touch of
teal in the room; wardrobe coordinated in navy, cream, charcoal and muted teal. Warm, real, collegial,
trustworthy mood. **Avoid:** text, logos, watermarks, distorted hands, neon or oversaturated tones,
harsh flash, stiff line-up group-photo posing, everyone staring at the camera.
