# method-cards.md — slots `vk-method-0`, `vk-method-1`, `vk-method-2`

**Aspect ratio:** 3:4 (portrait)
**Use:** Three "deliverable" document cards shown as tilted, floating cards on a **maroon** section
(`#7a1f2b`). Each is a tasteful close-up / flat-lay of a premium consulting deliverable. Each card a
**distinct** deliverable so the three read as a set, not duplicates. Keep them clean and tactile;
words on pages must be **unreadable / abstract** (no legible text), shown as elegant gray placeholder
lines, bars and charts.

> Reference the **House Style block** in `README.md`. Shoot all three with the same light, grade, lens
> and surface so they sit together as one set.

## `vk-method-0` — Strategy deck (printed slide pages)

### Variant A
Cinematic editorial flat-lay photograph, 3:4 portrait orientation, of a premium printed strategy deck
fanned on a warm cream paper surface (`#f5e1c8`): several thick matte slide pages showing elegant
abstract data layouts — clean bar charts, a simple line graph, a tidy grid of placeholder blocks, all
rendered as soft gray geometry with **no legible text**, only suggestive gray placeholder lines. A
navy hardcover folder edge and a brass paperclip add accents. Shot top-down, soft directional window
light from the left casting gentle long shadows, subtle film grain, full-frame 50mm macro at f/4,
crisp tactile detail of paper grain. Muted Vinika grade: deep navy shadows (`#07254c`), warm cream
and amber highlights (`#e0a045`), restrained cool mids. Premium, considered, tactile mood. **Avoid:**
legible text or real words, logos, watermarks, distorted shapes, oversaturated color, harsh flash,
clutter, plastic-looking paper.

### Variant B
Cinematic editorial close-up photograph, 3:4 portrait orientation, of an open strategy presentation
booklet on a desk, angled three-quarter view, pages showing abstract gray charts and a clean two-column
layout of placeholder bars (no readable text). A navy pen rests across the page; warm amber light
glances off the paper edge. Soft window light, film grain, full-frame 85mm prime at f/3.5, shallow
controlled depth. Muted navy-and-cream Vinika grade with amber highlight. **Avoid:** real words/text,
logos, watermarks, oversaturated tones, harsh flash, clutter.
