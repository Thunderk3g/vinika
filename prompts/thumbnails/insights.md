**Aspect ratio:** 4:3 (landscape)
**Use:** Editorial blog / journal header images, one per category. Abstract-but-meaningful — evoke the

arrows-up). Each header should still feel photographic and part of the Vinika set.

## `vk-insight-0` — Strategy

### Variant A
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. A quiet conceptual still: a single
architect's-style table by a large window where light rakes across an abstract arrangement of plain
navy and cream geometric blocks and a few thin amber lines forming an implied path or constellation —
suggesting planning and foresight without literal symbols. Soft directional daylight, long gentle
shadows, subtle film grain, full-frame 35mm prime at f/2.8, shallow depth and generous negative space
on one side for a headline. Muted Vinika grade: deep navy shadows (`#07254c`), warm cream/amber
highlights (`#f5e1c8` / `#e0a045`), restrained cool mids. Calm, intelligent, premium mood. **Avoid:**
visible text, logos, watermarks, lightbulbs, chess pieces, gears, arrows, distorted shapes,
oversaturated color, harsh flash, clutter.

### Variant B
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. Abstract overhead of a person's hands
(natural skin, correct fingers) arranging plain matte cards and a length of amber thread across a
cream surface into a deliberate, branching layout — the act of plotting a strategy, kept abstract with
no legible content on the cards. Soft window light, film grain, full-frame 50mm prime at f/4. Muted
navy-and-cream grade, amber accent line, negative space for a title. **Avoid:** readable text, logos,
watermarks, distorted hands, cliché icons, oversaturated color, harsh flash.

## `vk-insight-1` — Brand

### Variant A
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. A tactile abstract still of brand craft:
overlapping translucent color gels and paper swatches in navy, amber, teal and maroon fanned across a
cream surface, with soft light passing through them to cast layered colored shadows — evoking identity
and palette without any logo. Soft directional daylight, subtle film grain, full-frame 60mm macro at
f/4, crisp tactile texture, clean negative space to one side. Muted Vinika grade: navy shadows, warm
cream/amber highlights, cool mids, controlled saturation. Refined, design-forward, premium mood.
**Avoid:** visible text, logos, wordmarks, watermarks, rainbow oversaturation, distorted shapes, harsh
flash, clutter, literal paint-splash clichés.

### Variant B
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. Close, abstract macro of layered matte
paper edges and a soft gradient swatch transitioning from deep navy through teal to warm amber, with a
single mist-blue-grey curve crossing the frame — an evocative, premium impression of brand identity.
Soft window light, film grain, full-frame 90mm macro at f/4, shallow depth, negative space for a
headline. Muted Vinika grade, controlled saturation. **Avoid:** text, logos, watermarks, neon color,
distorted shapes, harsh flash.

## `vk-insight-2` — Operations

### Variant A
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. Abstract conceptual still of orderly flow:
clean parallel rows of small matte navy and cream objects (blocks / spools / tiles) precisely aligned
on a cream surface, with a single amber line threading through them — suggesting smooth, well-run
operations and process without literal gears or machinery. Soft directional daylight, gentle shadows,
subtle film grain, full-frame 35mm prime at f/3.2, generous negative space. Muted Vinika grade: deep
navy shadows, warm cream/amber highlights, cool restrained mids. Calm, precise, efficient, premium
mood. **Avoid:** visible text, logos, watermarks, gears, cogs, conveyor clichés, arrows, distorted
shapes, oversaturated color, harsh flash, clutter.

### Variant B
Cinematic editorial photograph, 4:3 landscape orientation. Overhead abstract of a person's hands
(natural skin, correct fingers) sliding matte tiles into neat aligned tracks on a cream surface, one
amber tile standing out in an otherwise navy-and-cream grid — evoking systems running in order. Soft
window light, film grain, full-frame 50mm prime at f/4, negative space for a title. Muted
navy-and-cream Vinika grade with amber accent. **Avoid:** readable text, logos, watermarks, distorted
hands, cliché machinery icons, oversaturated color, harsh flash.
